~A Profound Catharsis~

by Jean Dacudag Buniag

This is a collection of poems written since 2016.

Poetry is a form of literature that evokes a concentrated imaginative awareness of experience

or a specific emotional response through language chosen and arranged for its meaning, sound,

and rhythm. It is when an emotion has found its thought and the thought has found words.



"When power leads man toward arrogance, poetry reminds him of his limitations.

When power narrows the area of man's concern,

poetry reminds him of the richness and diversity of existence.

When power corrupts, poetry cleanses.""

~John F. Kennedy


When you find yourself drowning in the room full of clowns and nincompoops,

just take a deep breath, feel free to write a verse or two, and I assure you,

you won't need an inhaler or bid your loved ones adieu.







About Me

Greetings from the land of Cainta, Rizal 1900!
My name is Jean Buniag of the House Dacudag, First of My Name, the Unseen but Undead;
Queen of the Ghastly and Curious, the English Rockstar for a decade,
and, in an extremely unpopular opinion, a cool Millennial as my students said.
Molding minds for seven years is not always a blast,
Especially if the young minds are too stubborn to adjust;
Mind over matter has been my nuggin's crust,
However, mental health as the priority is truly a must!
Unbeknownst to me, I want to be a Junior Web Developer,
In the meantime, I succumb to poetry to express my thoughts, I gather;
and emotions that nobody will ever fathom, not even aglitter,
'Tis what I do since time immemorial, a journal in my imaginary aquifer.
I don't know much but this much I've known,
Poetry helps me not only in widening my vocabulary and affirmation,
but more importantly, to verbalize the depths of my emotion;
Forgive my nonsensicality for I have no means on what to write in this section.
